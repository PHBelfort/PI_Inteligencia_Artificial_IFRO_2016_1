Professional Ethics and Citizenship

A IMPORTÂNCIA DA ÉTICA NA COMPUTAÇÃO

Nowadays it has become essential for day to day activities whether it be corporate, educational, or they don't. Information technology has come to the homes, schools, businesses, revolutionizing education, work, and promoting aspects such as agility, security, interaction between people from different places.

The integration of information technology in business has improved so that they are better organized, and in addition to security and other benefits for herself, offering its guests quality service, responsive, precise. The job of the computer spreadsheet programs, text editors, software to search for information, database on the desktop accelerate the processes of activities generating a better offer of service.

Educational Informatics also revolutionized the way of learning through programs, dynamic, educational games. And, besides the distance education-DISTANCE EDUCATION, help in the integration of pupils from schools distant from major urban centres or in other countries, facilitating access to information.

With this huge computer revolution, as already mentioned earlier, there are many benefits, but no more offers to danger, to fraud. Since it is a relatively new area, but very comprehensive. However, escapes the control by not owning a certain code of professional ethics and so facilitates the employment of persons to work in it, and some of them have too much technical knowledge, however, without ethical abilities.

According to the explanation of Edson Barcaro and Emerson Freire, (2009) means a set of ethics rules acquired through culture, of education, of tradition and of daily life, which guide human behavior in a particular society.

Ethics is linked to the behavior of people on a daily basis. Professional Ethics is related to proceed as individual integrated to the practice of a profession. The most common problems involving pros are: computer influence in people's lives, copyright systems and software piracy.

There are organizations that try to, with codes of conduct, maintain morality in the area of information technology. This commitment is the Association for Computing machinery-ACM (Association for Computer Machinery) and another company which influences greatly on Community Informatics, in Brazil, is SBC, which consists of talented employees, including university professors, but the latter does not have a code set, the same is based on rules of the ACM and the codes of the British Computer Society , which is also an organization that ensures the good conduct in the field of Informatics, Edson Barcaro and Emerson Freire, (2009).

The ideal is to train professionals in both ethics and in the technical sense, because, one depends on the other to form a professional and not just a hacker, which can turn into cracker and disorders. This is why there is also importance of ethics in computing courses, to Polish the individual, making it the more ethical as possible, and added to the technical quality, be able to better serve the society.
