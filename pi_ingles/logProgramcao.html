Programming logic



What is programming logic? Is the technique of chain thoughts.

We use a logical series of steps to achieve a given purpose. Call, formally, this sequence Algorithm logic. Outside of computing, a classic example of algorithm is the cake recipe. In the recipe, have the step by step, the right measures and the proper ingredients for a meal, all in detail. In programming is no different, with respect to the algorithm. Knowing the purposes which will tend the program, are described step by step, all in a clear, concise, objective, and even logic.
